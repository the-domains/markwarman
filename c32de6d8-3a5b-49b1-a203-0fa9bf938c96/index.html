When I meet someone and the inevitable 'so what do you do?' question comes up, I'll respond that I'm a digital consultant. Depending on the crowd, the next question is often, 'Great! Can you fix my computer?' but, once I've explained myself a little more, I often get asked 'what's next?'.

This is a question I love. When $40,000 can help people who have been paralysed stand again or biodegradable blobs can replace plastic water bottles, when Amazon Prime Air aims to deliver orders within 30 minutes and the Grid can automatically design a website around the content you populate it with - my answer is 'anything'.
